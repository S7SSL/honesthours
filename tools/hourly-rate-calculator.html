How the Calculation Works

The calculator works backwards from your target take-home. It grosses up for tax, adds your expenses, then divides by your total billable hours to find your minimum break-even rate. The +25% buffer accounts for negotiation, dry spells, and non-billable time.

Billable hours tip: Most freelancers realistically bill 25–35 hours per week. The rest goes on admin, sales, and non-client work. Be honest about this when setting your rate.
