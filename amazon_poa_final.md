## PLAN OF ACTION — SUBMIT VIA "Submit new information" BUTTON

---

We are writing to appeal the removal of ASIN B0DFWQQJVM (ByErim Hair & Beard Scalp Scrub 200ml) under Amazon's Restricted Products Policy.

---

**Root Cause**

After carefully reviewing our listing and Amazon's Restricted Products Policy, we believe the restriction was triggered by one or more of the following:

1. The product name includes "Beard," which may have caused the listing to be incorrectly categorised as a product making claims related to hair growth or medicinal treatment of beard/scalp conditions — which was not our intent. The product is a cosmetic exfoliating scrub, not a medicinal or drug-related product.

2. The product listing may have contained language that could be interpreted as making claims beyond cosmetic benefit — for example, references to scalp health or condition improvement that exceeded what is permitted under Amazon's cosmetics guidelines for the UK marketplace.

---

**Corrective Actions Taken**

1. We have reviewed and updated the product title, bullet points, A+ content, and product description to remove any language that could be interpreted as a medicinal, drug, or scalp condition treatment claim.

2. All claims are now limited strictly to cosmetic benefits: exfoliation, cleansing, and scalp conditioning. No references to treating conditions, promoting hair growth, or reducing hair loss remain in the listing.

3. We have obtained and can provide the following compliance documentation for this product:
   - Cosmetic Product Safety Report (CPSR) confirming the product complies with UK Cosmetics Regulation (Retained EU Law 1223/2009)
   - Full INCI ingredient list demonstrating no restricted or prohibited ingredients are present above permitted thresholds
   - Certificate of Analysis from our manufacturer
   - Product safety data sheet

4. We have reviewed our entire product catalogue against Amazon's Restricted Products Policy and UK Cosmetic Regulations to ensure all remaining listings are compliant.

---

**Preventive Measures**

1. Going forward, all new product listings will be reviewed by a qualified Responsible Person under UK Cosmetics Regulation before being listed on Amazon.

2. We have implemented internal listing guidelines that prohibit any language that could be construed as medicinal, therapeutic, or drug-related — all claims are limited to cosmetic function only.

3. We have removed the word "Beard" from the product title to avoid any ambiguity about product categorisation — the product is a scalp scrub suitable for all hair types and scalp areas.

4. All product safety documentation (CPSR, MSDS, Certificate of Analysis) is now filed and available for immediate submission upon Amazon's request.

---

**Supporting Documentation Available**

We are prepared to submit immediately upon request:
- Cosmetic Product Safety Report (CPSR)
- INCI ingredient list
- Certificate of Analysis
- UK Responsible Person declaration
- Manufacturer safety documentation

We respectfully request reinstatement of ASIN B0DFWQQJVM. ByErim is a legitimate UK cosmetics brand committed to full compliance with Amazon's policies and UK regulatory requirements. This product presents no safety risk to consumers and has been updated to meet all Amazon listing requirements.
