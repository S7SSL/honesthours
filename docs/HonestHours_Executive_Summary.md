## Pricing

**£9/person/month**

**Justification:**
- Mid-market sweet spot (vs. Toggl £12-20, Clockify £5-10, Time Doctor £9.99)
- ROI on churn: Prevent one departure = 25-50x the cost
- ROI on morale: 5% productivity bump = 10-15x the cost
- ROI on compliance: Avoid one GDPR fine = 1000x+ the cost

**Model:**
- Per-employee (no hidden fees)
- 14-day free trial (no card)
- 30-day money-back guarantee
- Volume discount: 100+ employees = -15%
- Annual discount: -20%
