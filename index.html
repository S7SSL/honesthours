Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How does Honest Hours protect employee privacy?
+
We don't use invasive monitoring methods like screenshots or keystroke logging. Honest Hours works on the honor system—employees log their hours honestly, managers can trust the data. We encrypt all data in transit and at rest, and comply with GDPR and SOC 2 standards.
What happens after the 6-month free trial?
+
After 6 months, you can continue on any of our paid plans (£9/employee/month for Professional) or cancel anytime—no lock-in contract. You'll get 2 weeks' notice before your trial ends.
Can we integrate with our existing tools?
+
Yes! We integrate with Slack, Microsoft Teams, Jira, Asana, and more. Professional and Enterprise plans include API access for custom integrations.
Is there an admin panel for managers?
+
Absolutely. Managers get a full dashboard with team insights, custom reports, attendance tracking, and trend analysis. All non-intrusive, privacy-first metrics.
Do you offer onboarding support?
+
We offer email support on all plans and priority support for Professional and Enterprise customers. Enterprise customers get a dedicated account manager and custom training.
How secure is our data?
+
We use industry-standard encryption (AES-256), host on AWS with SOC 2 Type II compliance, and perform regular security audits. All data is backed up daily and can be exported anytime.
