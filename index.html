How it works

Up and running in minutes.

01
Install the extension
Employees install the Honest Hours Chrome extension. Takes 30 seconds. Opens a clean setup page — enter name, employee ID, and the company code your HR team provides.
02
Set your company code
HR or admin creates a company account at honesthours.io and gets a unique company code. Share it with your team. That's how employees link to your dashboard — no accounts for them to manage.
03
Start tracking honestly
Employees click Start when their day begins. Hours accumulate in real time. Manager sees who's active, when they clocked in, and total hours — updated live in the employer dashboard.
